# Custom ROM Comparison Matrix

Quick reference: what each major ROM focuses on.

| ROM | Focus | Base | Customization | Privacy | Performance | Support |
|-----|-------|------|---------------|---------|-------------|---------|
| **LineageOS** | Clean AOSP | AOSP | Heavy | Medium | Good | Excellent |
| **crDroid** | Feature-rich | AOSP | Extreme | Medium | Good | Good |
| **Evolution X** | Pixel-like | AOSP | Heavy | High | Excellent | Good |
| **GrapheneOS** | Security/Privacy | AOSP | Light | Excellent | Good | Pixel only |
| **CalyxOS** | Privacy | AOSP | Medium | Excellent | Good | Good |
| **/e/OS** | De-Google | AOSP | Medium | Excellent | Good | Good |
| **DivestOS** | Open Source | AOSP | Light | Excellent | Good | Good |
| **OmniROM** | Customizable | AOSP | Heavy | Medium | Good | Medium |

## When to pick each

- **Daily driver without fuss?** → LineageOS
- **Want every possible tweak?** → crDroid
- **Pixel experience, any device?** → Pixel Experience or Evolution X
- **Obsessed with privacy?** → GrapheneOS (Pixel) or CalyxOS
- **Want open source everything?** → DivestOS or CalyxOS
- **Balanced customization + privacy?** → Evolution X
