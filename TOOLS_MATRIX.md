## Decision Tree

**Want to customize home screen?** → Nova Launcher or Lawn Chair

**Want to root?** → Magisk (modules) or KernelSU (hiding)

**Want OS-wide theme?** → LSPosed + Iconify or Koinomi

**Want privacy?** → GrapheneOS or Netguard + F-Droid

**Want automation?** → Tasker (powerful) or Macrodroid (simple)

**Want screen mirror?** → Scrcpy (free) or Vysor (paid)

**Want to analyze apps?** → Frida (dynamic) or Jadx (static)

**Want clean ROM?** → LineageOS (stable, 250+ devices) or GrapheneOS (Pixel, max secure)
