# Android Launcher Comparison (2026)

| Launcher | Stars | Paid | Best For | Notable Features |
|----------|-------|------|----------|------------------|
| **Nova** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Yes | Power users | Highly customizable, desktop-like, gestures |
| **Niagara** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Yes | Minimalism | No home screen, card-based, privacy-focused |
| **KWGT** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Yes | Widgets | Infinite widget customization |
| **Lawnchair** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Free | Pixel feel | Google Pixel Launcher fork, Material You |
| **Microsoft Launcher** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Free | Productivity | Microsoft integration, timeline |
| **Siempo** | ⭐⭐⭐ | Free | Focus | Block ads, reduce distractions |
| **Poco Launcher** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Free | Bloat-free | Xiaomi's clean launcher |
| **Lunar Launcher** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Free | Dark-mode | OLED-friendly, minimal |
| **KISS** | ⭐⭐⭐ | Free | FOSS | Lightweight, search-first |
| **ZLauncher** | ⭐⭐ | Free | Gesture-heavy | All-gesture navigation |

---

**Recommendations:**

- **Maximum customization:** Nova + KWGT
- **Privacy + minimal:** Niagara + F-Droid
- **Stock Android feel:** Lawnchair or Pixel Experience
- **Minimal resource usage:** KISS or ZLauncher
- **Best all-rounder:** Nova (paid) or Lawnchair (free)
